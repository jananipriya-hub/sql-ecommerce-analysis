## Insights

1.Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-value customers, with customer C1 generating the highest spending.

2.The electronics category recorded the highest number of sales and generated the most revenue.

3.Credit card payments are the most frequently used payment method.

4.Sao Paulo is the top revenue-generating state.

5.February recorded the highest monthly revenue among the analyzed months.

6.Repeat customers significantly contribute to overall revenue.

7.Premium-priced products contribute substantial revenue despite lower sales volume.
